### About
Fast paced action filled arcade game that will let you take the role of an actor as they explore constantly changing themes and mediums of storytelling. Get ready for intense mask-switching gameplay that will not only change your character and their abilities, but also the stage and the enemies on it. Come play and try to beat your highscore.

Build wih [Godot](https://godotengine.org/).
